üíé Infinite Redux Stones

Using your massive wealth, you can now get as many Redux Stones as you need!

The Process

Travel to Capital of Mysteria and head to the Armor Shop on the eastern side of town.

Long Cape Method

Buy: Long Cape for 360 Dosh each (buy an EVEN number)

Trade: At Collector's Shop (western side) - each cape = 50 points

Cost: 100 points = 1 Redux Stone

Final Cost: 720 Dosh per Redux Stone

Since each Long Cape gives 50 points and you need 100 points for a Redux Stone, you need 2 capes per stone. That's why you buy an even number!

Repeat this process as many times as you like, but keep some Redux Stones aside for the remaking section.

üîÑ Remaking Method

For the final 3 Crafting Lives (Carpenter, Blacksmith, and Tailor), we use the Remaking method with our Infinite Redux Stones.

How Remaking Works

Remaking equipment changes the bonus stats randomly in exchange for Redux Stones. The amount of EXP gained depends on:

Equipment Rank
Higher rank = more EXP
Required Skill
Higher skill requirement = more EXP
Remaking Process
  1. Go to a crafting bench
  2. Select "Remake"
  3. Find the highest rank equipment for your target crafting life
  4. Remake it repeatedly using Redux Stones
  5. Gain massive EXP each time!
Pro Tip: This method can also be used for Alchemist or any crafting life that can remake equipment. It's especially useful when bulk crafting isn't viable.
